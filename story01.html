Special Accidents in Life Insurance

When the Financial Consultants introduce the product, they talk a lot about a special accident. The level of financial support for special accidents is 100%. Pay up to 200% in case of death or total permanent disability, and purchased together with Dai-ichi Life's premium Accident product. But very few people know or understand what a special accident in life insurance is like.

Special Accident is an accident that occurs in one or more of the following cases:

1. Customers who have purchased tickets are traveling on public transport including road, waterway, air. These vehicles must move regularly and have a fixed route. In this case, taxis and rental vehicles are not included as personal vehicles.

2. When the Customer is in a public elevator. Excluding escalators, elevators in mines and construction sites.

3. As a direct result of fires in public buildings. Including: Theaters, cinemas, halls, hotels, schools, hospitals, sports centers and commercial centers.

An accident in an apartment elevator is not called a Special Accident.
