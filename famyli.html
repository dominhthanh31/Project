1/ What is life insurance for the whole family?

Family life insurance is an insurance contract between the insurance company and the policyholder. This is a product that protects health, life, and ensures a solid financial foundation for the whole family in a single insurance policy. Therefore, life insurance not only has socio-economic significance but also has a profound human meaning.

2/ Should you buy life insurance for the whole family?

– Life insurance package for the whole family is the best health care solution for all family members against the risk of attack of dangerous diseases, especially cancer. With the support from insurance, your family will no longer have to worry about hospital fees if any member is sick or hospitalized for a long time.

- Life insurance is the best way to prevent dangerous risks: death, total and permanent disability. Relatives and children will still be protected and taken care of if the breadwinner in the family unfortunately passes away.

Life insurance provides peace of mind for the whole family. The comprehensive protection of insurance will help members always be financially proactive, always feel secure in work and in all activities. In other words, just one insurance policy, but the whole family will live happily every day.
