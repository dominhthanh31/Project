Guide to paying premiums for Dai-ichi Life Vietnam.

In order to create convenience and many options for customers to pay premiums to maintain the validity of the Contract, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam has developed many premium collection channels with diverse premium payment methods, completely free of charge. especially remote/online payment methods:

  • Dai-ichi Connect application or website http://kh.dai-ichi-life.com.vn (payment by domestic card or international card of more than 31 banks in Vietnam)
  • Internet banking/Mobile banking of the following banks:
  • VCB Digibank/ Vietcombank ATM
  • Insurance premium payment entrustment (Auto Debit) for customers with accounts at Sacombank.
  • E-wallet using Momo Wallet application.
  • E-wallet using Vi Viet application of Lien Viet Post Bank.
  • Deposit cash into Dai-ichi Life Vietnam's accounts of the following banks:
  • Transfer money to Dai-ichi Life Vietnam's account (transfer fee to be borne by the remitter).
  • Postal network of Vietnam Post Corporation nationwide (VNPost).
  • Transaction network of Viettel and Viettel supermarket (Viettel Store) nationwide.
  • Customer Service Centers at Dai-ichi Life Vietnam Offices/ General Agents (pay in cash or by domestic/international payment cards).

If you pay the fee via the Bank, please fill in the following details:

– Account holder: Dai-ichi Life Insurance Vietnam Co., Ltd

– Dai-ichi Life Vietnam account number

– Contents of payment: [Contract number]-[name of policyholder]-[phone number]
