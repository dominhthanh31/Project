Pay insurance premiums online with Dai-ichi Connect App

You log in to Dai-ichi Connect App. Select Online Premium Payment function to

  • Pay periodic insurance premiums
  • Refund the advance from the refunded value.
  • Refund the advance from the refund value for automatic fee payment by international cards (Visa, Master, JCB) and domestic payment cards issued by Vietnamese banks.

This is a completely free new payment service that helps you make transactions quickly, accurately, safely and conveniently.

Service to receive confirmation of periodic premium payment.

Easy, fast and accurate!

This service is available to all customers who have registered a mobile phone number with Dai-ichi Life Vietnam. So, Customers, please quickly contact the nearest Dai-ichi Life Vietnam office or call 0339202500, press 1 to register or update Dai-ichi Life Vietnam your mobile phone number so that you can update your mobile phone number. immediately use the benefits of this very convenient SMS service.

Utility 1: Periodically, you will be updated by Dai-ichi Life Vietnam on the status of your insurance policy (premium due, due date, ...) completely free of charge.

Utility 2: From 12/12/2012, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam will update you with a confirmation message of recurring premium payment completely free of charge.

Thus, you can know information about your contract as soon as a transaction arises without having to wait to receive information by mail.

Online insurance payment service.

Dai-ichi Life Vietnam customers can pay insurance premiums through the online banking channel of:

  • Bank for Foreign Trade of Vietnam Vietcombank (VCB iB@nking)

  • Bank for Investment and Development of Vietnam

  • Saigon Thuong Tin Commercial Joint Stock Bank (iBanking/mBanking)

1. Advantages.

Fast and convenient:

  • Within 5 minutes, you will be fully active in paying the premium for your policy with Dai-ichi Life Vietnam with just one click.
  • Transactions can be done anywhere with an Internet connection and at any time.
  • No commuting, no waiting in line.

Safe, Accurate and Confidential:

Through the application of a modern and secure information technology system:

  • Your premium payment will be safely and accurately transferred to Dai-ichi Life Vietnam.
  • Payment confirmation will be emailed to you right after the transaction is successful.
  • Your transaction information will always be kept strictly confidential.

2. Request.

To pay Dai-ichi Life Vietnam premium via internet banking, you need:

  • Have a current account at Vietcombank/ BIDV Bank, and
  • Register for VCB iB@nking Online Banking service at Vietcombank, register for BIDV online financial package service at BIDV / register for iBanking/mBanking service at Sacombank.

3. Payment of insurance premiums.

You only need to follow these 4 simple steps to pay Dai-ichi Life Vietnam premium:

  • Log in to VCB iB@nking Online Banking/ BIDV Online for individuals.
  • Select Payment/ Financial Services/ Dai-ichi Vietnam Life Insurance Company Limited (for VCB iB@nking); or select One-time bill payment/ Insurance collection/ Dai-ichi – Periodic premium payment or Dai-ichi – Other payment (for BIDV Online); select bill payment (for iBanking of Sacombank or Money transfer and payment / Invoice (for mBanking application).
  • Enter the contract number information, the fee payment amount according to the instructions.
  • Payment confirmation.
