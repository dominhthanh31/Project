INTRODUCE

Under Dai-ichi Life Holdings Inc. and a member of Dai-ichi Life Group, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam Co., Ltd. (Dai-ichi Life Vietnam) was established in January 2007, and this is the first overseas market. which Dai-ichi Life Group has 100% capital owned by Life Insurance Company.

After just over 12 years of operation, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam has grown 20 times in total premium revenue, built a solid foundation and maintained its position as one of the top 3 life insurance companies. in Vietnam with new insurance premium revenue of over VND 5,000 billion in 2018, reaching a total premium of over VND 11,500 billion and a market share of 13%. The company is honored to serve 2.5 million customers through a team of more than 1,300 employees and 92,000 professional financial advisors, proudly holding the third position in terms of customer service network with a system of 295 offices and nationwide agents (as of May 31, 2019).

On October 3, 2018, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam was approved by the Ministry of Finance for the eighth capital increase to nearly VND 7,700 billion, becoming one of the life insurance companies with large capitalization market, demonstrating strong financial potential as well as commitment to "Long-term commitment" with Vietnamese customers. On January 17, 2018, at the Reunification Hall of Ho Chi Minh City. In Ho Chi Minh City, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam solemnly held a ceremony to receive the Third Class Labor Medal awarded by the President to recognize the Company's outstanding achievements in contributing to the development of Vietnam's economy. as well as contributions to the community, especially in organizing and mobilizing support for the program to support poor patients. In addition to business activities, over the past 12 years, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam is proud to have pioneered many meaningful social charity activities with a total contribution of more than 27 billion VND. Dai-ichi Life Vietnam was honored twice to be awarded the "Excellent Enterprise for Social Responsibility" award by Asia Insurance Review, the first time in 2013 with the program "For the live a beautiful life” – providing clean drinking water systems for more than 24,000 students in schools in rural Vietnam, and the second time in November 2015 with the program “Million pink bricks, connecting happy bridges” – build 10 traffic bridges for people in remote areas across the country.

On June 2, 2017 in Bangkok, Thailand, Dai-ichi Life Vietnam was honored to be the only life insurance company in Vietnam to be awarded the "Asia Responsible Enterprise" award in 2017, awarded by the Asia Business Association. (Enterprise Asia) organizes and honors the excellent social responsibility of the Company through programs and community activities throughout, and the establishment of the Fund "For a beautiful life" in August 2016 has and is continuing its mission to bring better living values to Vietnamese people.

Dai-ichi Life has established a representative office in Vietnam since 2005.
